## 1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how Naju ("we," "our," or "the Application") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you use the Naju mobile application.

This version is intended to reflect the current observable behavior of the app. If we add features that materially change the data we process, we will update this policy.

**Company:** Naju  
**Country:** Argentina

---

## 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 2.1 Account and Authentication Information

When you register, sign in, or manage your account, we may collect and process:

- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number, if you choose to provide it
- Password information submitted by you for registration, sign-in, or password changes
- Account identifiers generated by our services, such as internal IDs and account/profile identifiers

### 2.2 Household, Preference, and Collaboration Information

To configure and operate the service for your household, we may collect and process:

- Household onboarding information, such as household composition, home type, pets, cooking habits, and related preferences
- Your active household code or active household profile
- Household profiles available to your account
- Email addresses of other users you authorize to access a household profile

### 2.3 Shopping and Household Management Data

To provide the core functionality of the app, we may collect and process information related to:

- Shopping lists
- Cart contents
- Pantry or inventory records
- Purchase or activity history related to household management
- Quantities, edits, confirmations, postponements, and other actions you record in the app
- Reasons or feedback you provide when editing, removing, or confirming items

### 2.4 Support and Messaging Integration Data

If you contact us or use optional messaging integrations, we may collect and process:

- Support message subject and content
- The status of your Telegram or WhatsApp account link
- Temporary codes or tokens required to complete those linking flows

### 2.5 Device, Session, and Local Storage Data

To keep the app working securely and conveniently, we may process:

- Access tokens and refresh tokens
- The email remembered for sign-in
- A locally generated `device_id`
- Your preference regarding biometric sign-in
- Whether you chose the "remember password" option
- If you enable "remember password," the app stores your password and a password hash in secure device storage so the app can facilitate future sign-in, including biometric-assisted sign-in when available

## 3. DEVICE FEATURES AND PERMISSIONS

In the current version of the app, we do **not** describe the app as requiring:

- location access;
- camera access;
- photo library access;
- contacts access; or
- microphone access.

The app may offer biometric authentication through the operating system if your device supports it and you choose to enable it.

The app may also open external services such as Telegram, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, or email clients when you choose to use those channels. Those third-party services operate under their own terms and privacy policies.

## 4. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information described above to:

- create and manage your account;
- authenticate you and maintain your session;
- save and apply your household preferences;
- display and synchronize your shopping list, cart, pantry, and related history;
- let you manage and share household profiles;
- provide support and respond to inquiries;
- enable optional messaging integrations when you request them;
- protect account security, prevent misuse, and troubleshoot service issues; and
- comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

## 5. SHARING OF INFORMATION

We may share personal information only when necessary to operate the service or comply with the law, including:

### 5.1 Service Providers

We may share information with service providers who help us host, operate, secure, or support the application and its backend services.

### 5.2 Messaging Platforms Chosen by You

If you choose to link Telegram or WhatsApp, we may exchange the minimum information needed to complete and maintain that link.

### 5.3 Legal Compliance and Protection

We may disclose information if required by law, valid legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and integrity of Naju, our users, or others.

### 5.4 What We Do Not Do

- We do not sell your personal data.
- We do not share your personal data with third parties for their own advertising purposes.

## 6. DATA RETENTION

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide the service, maintain security, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, and enforce our agreements.

Some information may remain in secure backups or archival systems for a limited period when deletion from those systems is not immediate.

Locally stored data on your device may remain until you sign out, disable remembered credentials, clear app data, uninstall the app, or delete your account.

## 7. DATA SECURITY

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal information.

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you cannot access the app and want your account or personal data deleted, you can use our public account deletion page:

- https://ww-ideas.github.io/naju-support/account-deletion.html

You may also request deletion by contacting us at:

- Email: soporte@naju.com.ar
- LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/company/naju/

To help us process your request, please contact us from the email associated with your account or include enough information for us to identify you securely.

When deletion applies, we will delete or anonymize the relevant personal data, except where retention is required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or backup-related reasons.
